Winning Your Traffic Violation Appeal

"Want to appeal a ticket, but don't know how to? Our mission is to encourage people to appeal their tickets and win with ease. The financial burden of some tickets may be great, and with our help, you will be able to avoid spending any money on unfair tickets."

Case Study: Anthony Ayala's Success in Appealing his Ticket

Anthony is a member of the community who works a job in managing construction projects. Despite Anthony's work load, he ensured his license plates were correctly displayed with an up-to-date plate and city sticker. However, nearing the expiration date of the car's plates, Anthony was unable to go to an Illinois Secretary of State facility to renew his plates due to work creating lack of time to do anything. Even though Anthony went 2 days before his plates expired to renew them, three days later he received a ticket for expired plates.

Anthony instantly thought that this ticket was unfair because he already filled out the necessary paper work to renew his plates and got caught in between the process. Even though Anthony was a little intimidated by the process, he prepared an argument demonstrating the issue was being resolved since he went to renew his plates 5 days before the ticket was issued and 2 days before the expiration date. When Anthony went to his designated court date, the presentation and detail of his argument convinced the judge that he was not liable of the ticket.

Therefore, Anthony's ability to confront the intimidation of the process by defending himself from an unfair ticket highlights that anyone can go into traffic court and contest their ticket. Even though Anthony had no background in law, he was able to construct an argument that helped him successfully appeal his ticket violation.
